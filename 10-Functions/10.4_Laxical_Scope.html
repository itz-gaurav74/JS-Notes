# Laxical Scope / Static Scope: - The Scope Of A Variable Is Defined By Its `Location Within The Source Code` And Nested Functions Have Access To Variables Declare In Their outer Scope. - Js Has Laxical Scope. - It Is The Scope Of Variable Or Function Based On Where It Is Defined n The Source Code. Example: O/p: a & b Accessible (Outer) : 10 20 a & b & c Accessible (Inner) : 10 20 30 Only a Is Accessible (Global) : 10
